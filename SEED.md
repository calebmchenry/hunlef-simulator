High fidelity simulator in the browser to practice the corrupted hunlef fight from old school runescape.

Same fight mechanics
Same game mechanics
Same assests
Same audio
Same tick system
Local in browser

Start at the beginning of the hunlef fight. Ignore all the pre work at the beginning of the guantlet.

Choose which tier armo you have: none, tier 1, tier 2, tier 3
Choose what bow you have: none, tier 1, tier 2, tier 3
Choose what staff you have: none, tier 1, tier 2, tier 3
Choose what halberd you have none, tier 1, tier 2, tier 3
Choose number of potions
Choose number of fish

Note: max inventory of extra weapons, food, and potions should be 28 to fit in the inventory.

Loop:
Start/Restart menu - where you can click to start or update your armor/inventory load out
Game simulation at the start of the corrupted hunlef fight
